1. Introduction

Welcome to MUA Planner ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

2.1 Account Information

Data Purpose
Email Address Account creation, login via OTP, notifications
Phone Number Optional alternative login via SMS OTP
Name Profile display, personalization
Profile Image Optional profile customization

2.2 Business Data

Data Purpose
Client Information Names, phone numbers, email addresses for event management
Event Details Dates, times, locations, event types, pricing
Team Members Contact details of your team for event coordination
Travel Records Travel bookings associated with events
Payment Information Package amounts, advance payments, balance tracking
Note: We do NOT collect or process actual payment transactions. Payment amounts are stored only for your record-keeping purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the MUA Planner service
  • Authenticate your account via email OTP
  • Send event reminder notifications
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Respond to your support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Session expiration after 30 days of inactivity
  • OTP-based authentication (no passwords stored)

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in your account
  • Update: Modify your profile and business data
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, use the Settings screen in the app or contact us directly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

MUA Planner is designed for business professionals and is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.
